Our Proprietary Protocol

The Salem Steamer Method

A defined, four-phase framework that governs every engagement — from initial assessment to final clearance. No improvisation. No assumptions. Only verified results.

I

Assessment Protocol

Comprehensive moisture mapping, thermal imaging, and air sampling establish an accurate, documented baseline — prior to any intervention. The assessment drives every subsequent decision.

II

Controlled Restoration

Surgical removal of compromised materials, containment barriers, and extraction systems are deployed with precision — minimizing disruption to surrounding areas while maximizing remediation efficacy.

Strategic Drying

Validation & Clearance

Our Foundation

Performance Over Marketing. Always.

Salem Steamer was founded in 2003 not to fill a market gap, but to meet a standard that did not yet exist in the Greater Washington area. Our founder recognized that property owners — especially those with high-value residences — were being served by a fragmented industry that competed almost entirely on price, with little accountability for technical outcomes or client experience.

Two decades later, that observation remains the engine of our operation. We are not the largest restoration company in the region. We are, by design, one of the most precise.

What Defines Us

Certifications That Are Earned, Not Displayed.

IICRC certification is not a marketing designation at Salem Steamer — it is the floor of our operational standards. Every technician is individually certified and subject to continuing education requirements. Our methods are aligned with published, peer-reviewed restoration standards: S500 for water damage, S520 for mold remediation, and NADCA specifications for HVAC systems.

When we certify a property as restored, that certification is backed by an independent industrial hygienist and a third-party certified laboratory — not our own internal assessment alone.

The Four-Phase Protocol

The Salem Steamer Method Applied to Water Damage

01

Precision Assessment

Thermal imaging and moisture mapping across all affected structures — walls, subfloors, ceiling cavities, and HVAC systems. Documentation is generated before any extraction begins, establishing a defensible baseline for your insurance claim and our restoration plan.

02

Surgical Extraction & Controlled Demolition

Industrial truck-mounted and portable extraction units remove standing and absorbed water. Where structural materials are unsalvageable, controlled demolition is performed to defined perimeters — preserving as much of the original structure as possible while eliminating microbial risk.

03

Strategic Drying

Desiccant and refrigerant dehumidifiers, high-velocity air movers, and in-wall drying systems are deployed in configurations calculated from psychrometric data — not intuition. Daily monitoring and documentation ensure materials reach IICRC-specified moisture content targets before any reconstruction commences.

04

Validation & Third‑Party Clearance

An independent industrial hygienist conducts post-restoration air sampling and structural moisture verification. Certified laboratory analysis confirms the property has been restored to pre-loss conditions. You receive a signed clearance report — suitable for insurance submission, real estate disclosure, or personal assurance.

The Six Pillars of Discretion

Every Layer of Privacy,
Fully Activated.

01

No Paper Trail. No Gossip.

All communications are conducted through encrypted channels. Written reports are issued only in formats and to recipients you specify. Internal references to your engagement exist under a code name assigned at intake — never your name or address. Staff are bound by institutional non-disclosure policy from the moment of assignment.

02

No Neighborhood Watch.

Our Phantom Fleet vehicles arrive in unmarked, unremarkable vehicles — no company branding, no logos, no magnetic signage. Arrival windows can be coordinated to minimize the presence of neighbors or curious passersby. We are invisible by design.

03

For Your Eyes Only.

Reports — including our internal documentation, the public adjuster's findings, and the independent hygienist's clearance — are sealed and issued to you alone. No copies are retained without your written instruction. No third parties receive documents without your signed authorization.

04

Your Home's History Remains Yours Alone.

We do not maintain permanent records of the nature of your service beyond what is legally required and explicitly agreed upon. The restoration of your property does not become part of any database, referral pool, or case study without explicit written consent from you.

05

Stealth Uniforms. Professional Appearance.

Our technicians arrive in plain professional attire — no company branding, no visible identification. To your neighbors, they could be any number of private household professionals. Their conduct on your property is governed by the same exacting standards that define all Salem Steamer work.

06

Restore Your Inner Peace.

Beyond the physical restoration, the Discretion Premium is designed to eliminate the secondary distress of visibility — the worry that neighbors, acquaintances, or future property valuators will become aware of an incident that is, ultimately, no one else's concern.
